## Architecture

- ESP32 - reflexes, safety, movement, survival.  
- Rpi - local autonomy.
- PC - character, planning.
-----
- ESP32 survives in the absence of the Rpi.
- Rpi survives in the absence of the PC.
-----
- The Rpi interfaces to the PC over WiFi.
